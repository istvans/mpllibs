list monad_plus
===============
Abel Sinkovics

This is the C++ template metaprogramming equivalent of Haskell's List monad
plus.

general information
-------------------

* header: +<mpllibs/metamonad/list.hpp>+
* tag of monad plus: +list_tag+
* mzero: empty list
* mplus: list concatenation
